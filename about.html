Our Story

We create spaces that feel intentional at every scale.

Genkin Enterprises began with a simple ambition: build better, communicate clearly, and deliver work that still feels right long after handover. Since then, the company has grown into a trusted Nairobi-based partner for residential, institutional, hospitality, and renovation projects.

Our approach combines technical discipline with a careful eye for finish. We believe construction should solve practical needs, elevate everyday experience, and leave behind a result that clients are proud to own.
