About Genkin

We build spaces that feel composed, practical, and lasting.

Genkin Enterprises is a Nairobi-based civil engineering and building company known for combining technical discipline with a careful eye for finish. We work across residential, education, hospitality, and renovation projects with the goal of delivering spaces that still feel right long after handover.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the brief clearly, coordinate the work properly, and execute with precision. That balance of practicality and polish is what continues to define the Genkin standard.

KENYA YETU

Nairobi is one of the youngest cities in Africa which is still standing tall. Architects and planners agree that it is a "living heritage city" where the city planning system is still based on the ancient traditional principles. Nairobi is a booming city and it is one of the front runners to become the most powerful industrial city of Africa. The rate at which it is growing, the city is now witnessing a constant stream of investors from all over the world.

So, it's time that the world looks at Nairobi as a success story of growth.
